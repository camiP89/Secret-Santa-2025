About the Secret Santa Game 🎅

This Secret Santa game was based on an idea from Harriet. Each player is secretly assigned someone to give a gift to, making the exchange fun and surprising.

🎁 How it Works

  1. Each player’s name appears as a button on the Secret Santa page.
  2. When it’s your turn, click your name to reveal who you’re giving a gift to.
  3. Once you reveal your pairing, all other names become locked 🔒.
  4. You’ll only be able to view your match once — no takebacks!
  5. Even if you refresh or reopen the page, the game will stay locked to keep it secret.

🛠️ Behind the Scenes

The game uses a bit of JavaScript magic and localStorage to keep everyone’s pairings consistent and private. Once someone reveals their match, the results are saved, and the game is disabled for everyone else.

🎄 Have fun, keep it secret, and don’t spoil the surprise!
